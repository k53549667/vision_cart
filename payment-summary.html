🔄 How Payment Flow Works

Step 1: Add to Cart
Customer adds products to shopping cart
Step 2: Proceed to Checkout
Click cart icon → "Proceed to Checkout" → Redirected to checkout.html
Step 3: Enter Details
Fill delivery address, phone, email
Step 4: Select Payment
Choose payment method and enter required details
Step 5: Place Order
Click "Place Order Securely" → Order processed
Step 6: Confirmation
Success message displayed, order saved, cart cleared
